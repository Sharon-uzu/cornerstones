Prime Cornerstone Afro Initiative is a social development organization, we are committed to creating African solutions to African problems; mobilizing and utilizing African resources in solving them. We work with volunteers, individuals, organizations, government agencies and communities to implement the Integrated Community Intervention Programme model (ICIP) which delivers health, education, economic empowerment, environment, culture, aid/relief solutions to communities. Our solutuions are funded by donations from individuals and organisations with a passion to see a prosperous Africa. 100% of all donations funds Africa’s Development. Prime Cornerstone Afro Initiative (PCAI) is an Afrocentric non-governmental organization committed to building Africa from the ground up, one individual, one community at a time. Our work is guided by the values of inclusiveness, accountability, transparency, innovation, excellence.

Dr. Starr Horsfall

Dr. Starr Horsfall is a father, and son of the kalabari and Ikwerre kingdoms in Rivers State Nigeria. A medical doctor with decades of clinical experience . He has a masters degree in business administration, and a masters in healthcare administration. He is a philanthropist, humanitarian, businessman, and a motivational speaker with roots in the kalabari. Ikwerre and etche kingdoms in Rivers State, Nigeria. On his maternal side, he has roots in the Yoruba kingdom and Sierra Leone.

He serves Prime Cornerstone Afro Initiative as the chairman and CEO. He is an avid traveler, physical fitness enthusiast and a proud father. He believes in an Africa that was great and the Africa that will be even greater.
