Our Approach

The problems bedeviling communities in Africa are enormous and interrelated. As an organization our interventions are designed to integrate solutions in the areas of health, education, economic empowerment, environment, culture, aid and relief into one program which would be implemented holistically in every community we work in.

The model recognizes that development issues are interrelated as they are causes or effects of/for each other i.e, it recognizes that poor state of health can be a cause for poverty which is an economic issue or poor access to quality education can be an effect of poverty etc. It is inline with this realization that our interventions are designed to provide and deploy solutions in our six intervention areas simultaneously for every community we work in.

Moving Communities and Individuals from one point of Development to others holistically

With a population of over 1.3 billion people, an enormously rich cultural heritage, history and abundant human and natural resource, the African continent remains under developed. Prime Cornerstone Afro Initiative is on a mission to change this narrative by creating African solutions, mobilizing and utilizing African resources to build Africa from the ground up.
