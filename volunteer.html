Africa Builders Community

Who we are.

The Africa Builders Community is the volunteer arm of Prime Cornerstone Afro Initiative. It is a hub of volunteers from all over Africa dedicated to supporting the effort to build Africa from the ground up, one community, one individual at a time.

What we do.

The Africa Builders Community provides a support strucuture for PCAI to implement the integrated community intervention model in communities across Africa.

ABC members are Involved in project design, development, implementation, monitoring and evaluation, based on areas of interests and skillset.

Why Join Us

The Africa Builders Community is made up of volunteers from all over Africa committed to driving positive impact across the globe. We are resilient, cultured and deliberate individuals sharing care, love and hope while building individuals and communities around us.

As volunteers, we have the opportunity to work in many aspects of the organization after receiving a comprehensive orientation followed by additional training to prepare us for our chosen volunteer role.

Volunteer time is extremely valuable to us, and we strive to make the volunteer experiences at Prime Cornerstone Initiative fun, inspiring, engaging and educational
